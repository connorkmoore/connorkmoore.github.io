## Overview

Pioneered the adoption of Ceptah Bridge to link JIRA burn-down velocity directly to SAP time-charging, creating a real-time data link between engineering effort, budget health, and customer commitments.

## Technologies Used

- JIRA
- SAP
- MS Project
- Ceptah Bridge

## Timeline

May 2023 - Present

## Impact

This integration standardized Agile metrics company-wide, aligning sprint velocity with contractual delivery obligations and providing real-time visibility into budget health and project status.
